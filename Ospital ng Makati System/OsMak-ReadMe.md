# 🏥 Ospital ng Makati (OsMak) System: Healthcare QA
**Domain:** Health Informatics | Hospital Management System (HMS) | Enterprise ERP

The OsMak System is a mission-critical healthcare platform designed to manage patient records, clinical workflows, and hospital administrative data. As the QA Lead, I spearheaded the quality assurance process to ensure the system met the high reliability and security standards required for public healthcare services.

---

## 🛠️ My Role & Responsibilities
I managed the end-to-end testing lifecycle for the OsMak platform, focusing on the seamless integration of medical modules and the protection of sensitive patient data.

### 🔍 Key QA Focus Areas:
* **Clinical Workflow Validation:** Verified complex end-to-end patient journeys, from registration and triage to consultation, laboratory requests, and billing.
* **Data Privacy & Security:** Ensured that patient information remains confidential and accessible only to authorized medical personnel through rigorous role-based testing.
* **Module Integration Testing:** Validated the synchronization between different hospital departments (e.g., Pharmacy, Radiology, and Records) to prevent data silos or errors.
* **System Reliability:** Conducted stability testing to ensure the platform remains performant during peak hospital hours and high-volume data entry.

---

## 🧪 Testing Methodology
Healthcare software requires a "Zero-Error" approach. I implemented the following strategies to maintain system quality:

1. **Functional Module Audit:** Performed deep-dive manual testing on individual hospital modules to ensure specific clinical requirements were met.
2. **End-to-End (E2E) Scenario Testing:** Simulated real-world hospital scenarios to verify that data flows correctly from the emergency room to the discharge counter.
3. **Boundary & Input Validation:** Tested medical data entry forms (e.g., vitals, prescriptions) for strict input validation to prevent clinical errors caused by incorrect data.
4. **Regression Testing:** Maintained a robust regression suite to ensure that hospital-wide updates did not disrupt existing critical medical services.

---

## 📂 Documentation Standards
All testing activities were documented with clinical-grade precision using standardized **Proof of Testing (POT)** formats. 

* **Healthcare Test Scripts:** Detailed step-by-step verification of clinical features.
* **Defect Management Logs:** Managed via an organized ticketing system to prioritize life-critical vs. administrative bugs.
* **Compliance Reports:** Maintained logs to demonstrate system readiness for hospital-wide deployment.
